About Us

Bridging Native Enterprise & Federal Procurement

Sovereign Contracts Group, LLC is a Native American–owned Indian Small Business Economic Enterprise (ISBEE) founded to deliver exceptional products and services to federal agencies. We operate within the Buy Indian Act procurement framework, one of the most structurally advantaged federal market channels available to Native American firms.

What is an ISBEE?

An Indian Small Business Economic Enterprise (ISBEE) is an Indian Economic Enterprise (IEE) that also qualifies as a small business under SBA size standards. Federal contracting officers must give priority to ISBEEs for all purchases, regardless of dollar value, through set-aside or sole-source awards.

Buy Indian Act

The Buy Indian Act is a statutory preference authority requiring covered agencies to use Buy Indian procurement “whenever practicable.” Within DOI Indian Affairs (BIA and BIE), this means ISBEE consideration comes first—and departing from this preference requires a formal written deviation.
